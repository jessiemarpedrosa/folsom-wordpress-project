KNOW MORE ABOUT US

Michael Folsom, a third generation Contractor and forth generation Floridian, began Folsom with the strong core values passed down from his Grandfather and Father. The most important of those values in Michael’s opinion is doing everything possible, as a team, to exceed each client’s expectations throughout every project. Michael and his partners have built a team of professionals to ensure Folsom does just that. Michael’s passion for the business and upholding his family’s legacy give him the drive to push Folsom forward to diversify as a company and continually strive to improve business processes in order to provide the best value to their clients.

Our Services

GENERAL CONTRACTING

The traditional method of project delivery, where the owner/developer maintains the responsibility for choosing a design, selecting a design professional and a contractor. The General Contractor assumes responsibility for the organization and execution of the construction activities throughout the project. Folsom utilizes subcontractors for all labor performed on a project.

View our Works >

DESIGN BUILD

The heart of the design/build approach is the establishment of a single source of management and accountability, typically with the general contractor. The Folsom Team has repeatedly proven the value of the design/build approach in maintaining the fiscal integrity of a project, while meeting financing criteria, critical quality standards and accelerated scheduling commitments.

TRUST FOLSOM

Simply put, if we bid, developers win.

We pride ourselves on the Folsom “bid to win” process which includes understanding of the goals of the development, the fine details in the bid documents, the current subcontractor environment and knowledge of the strengths and weaknesses of the all members. Even if we lose the bid, we increase the quality of the competition and assuming the winning construction group executes well, the developer ultimately wins.

Post bid, we keep our promises.

For three generations, Folsom has delivered. We take pride in our accident free safety record, staying within the budget and creating repeat clients. It’s why 85% of our assignments come referrals.
